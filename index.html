Shenzhen2020

A science-nonfiction film by Maya Rudolph & Xiaowei Wang

  • About

In China's ubiquitous technosphere, a self-identified cyborg, an idealistic engineer, a millionaire farmer, and a sci-fi writer's lives are transformed by the Internet and the vanishing space between imagined futures and real life as we approach the year 2020.
